Becoming a Member

If you want to become a member of our club and participate in all our activities, please fill out this form. We need this information to contact you and register you as a member. Please note that if you are not taking any credit classes, you will not be able to go with us on field trips sponsored by the college or receive giveaways or prices. This is because students taking non-credit classes don't pay a fee that the college uses to finance student activities. However, you are welcome to join our general meetings.
