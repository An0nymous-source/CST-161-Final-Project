Mission Statement

The club aims to connect students from different cultural backgrounds, nationalities, and heritage. The goal is to promote and celebrate the diversity that includes all cultures and to connect on a human level regardless of our differences.

Vission Statement

We believe our club provides a space where students have the opportunity to share their culture and traditions, which helps to break the stigmas of prejudice, racism, and discrimination.

Club Rules

  • Everyone must comply with the SCCC - Code of Conduct
  • Everyone is encouraged to share their cultures and traditions; however, we must be careful when speaking about sensitive topics such as religions and politics.
  • We must not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, religion, creed, age, disability, sex, gender identity, sexual orientation, familial status, pregnancy, predisposing genetic characteristics, military status, domestic violence victim status, or criminal conviction.
